### What I do

Most tools tell you a token's *price*. I build the ones that tell you its *intent*.

I work at the layer where raw chain data becomes evidence — behavioral risk scoring,
token forensics, deployer reputation, and the compliance tooling that turns "looks
sketchy" into a defensible verdict. I started on Solana, the hardest adapter, and I'm
making it chain-agnostic from there.

I care about one thing: being the honest broker in a dirty room. Calibrated findings,
evidence over assertion, no slop.

---

### 🔥 RugBurn — behavioral risk &amp; token forensics

[**RugBurn**](https://rugburn.io) is a Solana-native engine that scores tokens on how
they *behave*, not just how they're configured. Structure lies; behavior is harder to
fake. It reconstructs liquidity dynamics, deployer timing, trade authenticity, and
lifecycle staging into a single risk verdict — with append-only, hash-chained evidence
behind every call.

- **Behavioral, not structural** — SPL tokens are structurally identical, so the signal
  lives in flow dynamics and operator behavior.
- **Evidence-grade** — append-only hash-chained event log; every verdict is auditable.
- **Built to scale across chains** — adapter pattern over a normalized transfer/entity
  graph, so forensics follow the money instead of stopping at one chain's border.
- **Track record** — two top-5 finishes in the BirdEye On-Chain Intelligence hackathon,
  scoring 10/10 across nearly every technical category.

> Currently extending RugBurn into a chain-agnostic forensics core and shipping
> open-source compliance primitives for the Solana and Stellar ecosystems.
